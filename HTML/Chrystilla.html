Biography and Background

Chrystilla, a dedicated and ambitious student, is in her third year of a BSc in Quantity Surveying at the University of Pretoria. Born and raised in the Eastern Cape, she developed a strong passion for the built environment and is determined to make an impact in the construction industry. Now in her final years of study, Chrystilla is honing her skills in cost management, project planning, and sustainability. She embraces the challenges of her degree with resilience and a strong work ethic, striving for excellence in everything she does. With her dedication and vision for the future, she is set to build a successful career in the field of Quantity Surveying.
