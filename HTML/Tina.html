Biography and Background

Tina Magqira was born and raised in Cape Town, where her curiosity and problem-solving nature shaped her from a young age. Inspired by resilience and adaptability, she has always embraced change as an opportunity for growth. She began her academic journey at the University of Pretoria (UP), initially enrolling in a BSc in Quantity Surveying. However, within her first year, she realized that the field wasn’t her passion. Determined to pursue something more aligned with her interests, she made the bold decision to switch programs. Tina is now studying for a BIT in Information Systems, majoring in Information Science. This shift allowed her to explore her love for technology and problem-solving, opening doors to new opportunities in the digital and business world. Her journey reflects her willingness to take risks and carve out a path that truly excites her.

Hobbies

Outside of academics, Tina enjoys going to the gym to stay active and relieve stress. Fitness plays a key role in her daily routine, helping her maintain discipline and balance. She is also an avid reader, constantly exploring new books that expand her knowledge and perspective, whether in technology, self-improvement, or fiction.
